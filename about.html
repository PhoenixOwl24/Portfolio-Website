About Me


Hello! My name is Kaila, and I'm passionate about capturing moments and crafting stories through various media. I currently work at a vibrant tattoo shop, where I serve as the social media marketer. My role allows me to blend creativity with strategy, showcasing the artistry of our talented tattooists through engaging and visually appealing content.

Behind the camera is where I truly come alive. I'm skilled in photography, videography, and editing, which lets me transform ideas into stunning visuals that resonate with our audience. Whether I'm snapping a candid shot, filming an intricate tattoo process, or piecing together a compelling video, I strive to bring a unique and authentic perspective to every project.

I am also pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Communication Design at City Tech. This educational journey enhances my understanding of visual storytelling and design principles, enriching the content I create both professionally and personally.

When I'm not immersed in work or studies, you can find me indulging in my hobbies. I'm an avid sticker collector, always on the lookout for unique and quirky designs to add to my collection. I also have a deep love for reading, constantly exploring new worlds and ideas through the pages of a book.

I believe in the power of visual communication and am dedicated to honing my craft, whether it is behind the camera, in front of a computer editing, or through the lens of my studies. I'm excited to continue growing as a content creator and designer, sharing my journey and passions along the way.
